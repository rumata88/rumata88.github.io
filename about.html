About Me

Hi! My name is Vera Reznik and I’m Vancouver-based food photographer and food stylist, specializing in natural style photography for restaurants, food magazines, food advertising and commercial & editorial food photography.

I was born and grew up in Ukraine and I was always felt in love with the amazing colourful and tasteful foods and emotions of people who create it.

I work in Vancouver and its surroundings and I speak Russian, Hebrew, Ukrainian, and English. That’s why we can always understand each other and to realize all our plans and ideas!
